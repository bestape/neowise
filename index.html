Problem

Foundational DeFi and legal-formalization technologies are rare and hard to teach. Teams can't spin up experts quickly to implement first-principles AMMs, uxNFT-enabled frontends, or legally-traceable contract decisions. Neowise solves this by becoming the trained expert — one AI pupil that trains teams and ships production-ready outputs.

Solution

Hybrid knowledge stack

Combine Wolfram-style symbolic math with LLM engineering to produce deterministic, testable, and explainable outputs for AMMs and financial product design.

Patent-informed training

Train on the "calculus computing" patent family and SeedTreeDB syntax so generated code and decisions are traceable and compatible with sui generis IP claims where relevant.

Developer-first outputs

Ship code, test suites, mathematical proofs, and uxNFT frontends that integrate with Orbswap and streaming token primitives.

Use cases

  • Automated AMM design & parameterization for Orbswap-like markets.
  • Realtime streaming-token stablecoin tooling (nerite-style streams).
  • uxNFT frontends for DeFi with built-in legal traceability (ERC-7827 / SeedTree).
  • Training programs: make Neowise the instructor for on-boarding engineering teams.
